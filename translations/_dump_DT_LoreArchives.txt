What is Apex?

After years of fear and uncertainty against the whims of mother nature, modern cience finally offers a suitable solution to all american citizens: Apex corporation brings up state of the art technology capable of predicting dangerous weather events, as well as protocols to control the damage that these may cause to the population and property.
